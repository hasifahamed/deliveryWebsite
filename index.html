FAQs

Got Questions?

What areas do you deliver in? +
We currently cover Chennai and surrounding neighborhoods. WhatsApp us with your pickup and drop location to confirm coverage in your area. We're constantly expanding!
How do I place an order? +
Simply WhatsApp us at 9566700534 with your pickup location, drop location, and what you need delivered. We'll confirm availability and pricing instantly.
How long does delivery take? +
Most deliveries within 5km are completed in 30–45 minutes. Longer distances may take slightly more time. We'll give you a real-time ETA when you order.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash on delivery, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and bank transfers. Payment is collected after delivery is completed.
Do you deliver medicines and documents safely? +
Absolutely. Medicine and document deliveries are treated with the highest priority and confidentiality. We handle them with special care and deliver directly to the recipient.
